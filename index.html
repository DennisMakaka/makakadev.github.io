The Maze

A 3D navigable maze rendered with raycasting techniques.

Explore The Maze

Key Features

Raycasting Rendering Engine

The core of The Maze project is its custom-built raycasting engine that renders a 3D environment in real-time from a 2D map.

Collision Detection

Our precise collision detection ensures realistic interaction with the environment, preventing users from clipping through walls.

Smooth Camera Movement

Navigate the maze effortlessly with smooth camera transitions and responsive controls.
